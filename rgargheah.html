Red Dead Redemption 2

Red Dead Redemption 2 is a realistic story game made in october 26 2018, The story is set in the US in 1899 and follows the exploits of the main character Arthur Morgan, outlaw and member of the Van der Linde gang, who deals with the decline of the Wild West while attempting to survive against government forces and rival gangs.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite.

  • the story is really really good.
  • the characters are very unique.
  • the game is very realistic.
  • the attention to detail is very good.
  • the game reflects the way you act and the choices you make throughout the story.
